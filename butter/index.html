How to butter coffee, with pictures.

Step #1

Butter goes into mixing vessel. Maybe just over 1 Tbsp. (Coffee brewing process has begun at this point, too.)

Step #2

Add coconut oil. Two or three generous spoonfuls (or so).

Step #3

Top view at this stage, for perspective.

Step #4

I add half and half, about this much.

Step #5

I add sriracha hot sauce. Every step is optional, but this step is strictly optional.

Step #6

About this much sriracha.

Step #7

Add coffee. I use about 8 oz of coffee, give or take. A total amount between 350 and 400 mL is perfect for this particular mixing container.

Step #8

The view immediately before blending.

Step #9

A splash guard (well used) for the immersion blender.

Step #10

It blends in a couple seconds. This is the result.

Step #11

Pour & enjoy!
